### 4. Update Pricing Tiers (User-Specified Pricing)

**User Requirements:**

**iPhone (9 pagi - 8 malam):**
- 0-50 jam: Rp 65,000/jam
- 51-70 jam: Rp 60,000/jam
- 71-120 jam: Rp 58,000/jam
- 121-150 jam: Rp 55,000/jam
- 151+ jam: Rp 50,000/jam

**Camera+OBS (9 pagi - 8 malam):**
- 0-70 jam: Rp 120,000/jam
- 71-140 jam: Rp 115,000/jam
- 141-200 jam: Rp 105,000/jam
- 201+ jam: Rp 95,000/jam

**Note:** These pricing tiers are slightly cheaper than package prices to encourage custom bookings.

**Package Price Comparison:**
- **iPhone:** 60,000 - 75,000/jam (depending on package size)
- **Camera+OBS:** 93,590 - 125,000/jam (depending on package size)

## Notes

- **Countdown Timer:**
  - Fixed 24-hour period for demo purposes
  - For production, consider making it configurable or based on actual promo end time

- **Pricing Strategy:**
  - Custom jam pricing is slightly CHEAPER than package pricing to encourage flexible bookings
  - This is opposite of initial plan - custom jam is now more competitive
  - Users can choose between "Paket Jam" (fixed packages) and "Custom Jam" (flexible hours)

- **Pricing Tiers:**
  - iPhone: Rp 50,000 - 65,000/jam (5 tiers)
  - Camera+OBS: Rp 95,000 - 120,000/jam (4 tiers)
  - All tiers apply to operational hours (9 pagi - 8 malam)

- **UI Behavior:**
  - PackageSelection component already has tabs for "Paket Jam" vs "Custom Jam"
  - Custom jam input allows users to specify total hours
  - System automatically calculates days based on 2 hours per session
  - Minimum 6 days (12 hours) for custom bookings
