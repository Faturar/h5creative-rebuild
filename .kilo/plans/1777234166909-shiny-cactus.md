### Pricing Calculation Flow

1. **Custom Booking:**
   - User selects device type and total hours
   - System determines pricing tier based on total hours
   - User selects time slots in TimeSlotSelection
   - System calculates surcharges based on time slots
   - Final price = (hourly rate × total hours) + total surcharges
   - Displayed in PackageSelection (estimated) and BookingSummary (final)

2. **Package Booking:**
   - User selects package (fixed price)
   - Package price is used directly
   - No tiered pricing or surcharges applied
   - Displayed in PackageSelection and BookingSummary
